KAIZER CHIEFS HISTORY

Kaizer Chiefs Football Club (FC) is a South African football club that was founded in 1970.
The club is known for its rich history and many trophies won.

                
Founding 

                
Kaizer Chiefs FC was founded in January 1970 by Kaizer Motaung, a former soccer player.
                Motaung was born in Orlando East, Soweto on October 16, 1944.
                He played as a striker for the Atlanta Chiefs in the North American Soccer League (NASL).
                Motaung combined his first name with the Atlanta Chiefs to create the name of Kaizer Chiefs.

                
                
Trophies 

                
Chiefs FC is the most decorated club in South African football.
                They have won the League Championship 12 times, the National Cup 13 times, and the League Cup 13 times.
                They are also one of only three teams in South Africa to win a CAF competition.
