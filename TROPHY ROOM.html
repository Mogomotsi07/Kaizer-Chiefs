Kaizer Chiefs, the most decorated club in South African football, have won 93 trophies over the past 47-year history.

The Gold-and-Black have won the League Championship twelve times, lifted the National Cup on 13 occasions, with fifteen top 8 titles (the most by any team in SA), have won the League Cup thirteen times and several unofficial cups.

They are also one of only three teams in South Africa to ever win a CAF competition, defeating Interclube (Angola) in 2001 to lift the Cup Winners Cup (better known as the CAF Confederations Cup).

Amakhosi were also awarded the African team of the year that same year.
